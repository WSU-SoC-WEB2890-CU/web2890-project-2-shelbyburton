Welcome to My Small Kitchn ~


I’ve had the good fortune to have had a large kitchen, which I loved! Now, however, my kitchen is much smaller, and regardless of the size, I want my kitchen to function in the same manner –
And while, that isn’t always possible, I’ve gotten creative and am still able to have fun and create wonderful memories in the heart of my home.
My goal is to help you do the same, by sharing resources, tips & tricks etc. and saving you time, energy and money along the way ~
